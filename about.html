Welcome to my portfolio website! I designed this mock website to display some of my photography work from my photography class. This site was developed from scratch in Dreamweaver using HTML, CSS and Bootstrap (Did I forget to mention that I am a Web Designer, too?). The photos were taken with a 35mm fully manual camera using Kodak TRI-X, ISO400 film and were developed in a dark room at the Palm Beach State College campus. What I love about using a vintage camera is how raw the images turn out. Photos that are in their natural state without being Photoshopped are more authenthic and I can better appreciate them. There are a variety of subject matter in this portfolio but my preference is taking photos of nature.
